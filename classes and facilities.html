BODY PUMP (strength and conditioning)

Body pump is a fast-paced, barbell-based workout that's specifically designed to help you get lean, toned and fit. It ised a combination of motivating music, fantastic instructors and scientifically proven moves to help you achieve your #newyear resolutions more quickly than you would workout on your own.

BODY COMBAT, high energy

Punch and kick your way to fitness in this high-energy martial-arts inspired workout. The class is completely non-contact, with easy-to-follow moves inspired by a wide array of mixed martial arts, such as karate, boxing, taekwondo, tai chi and MuayThai. With each round, you’ll be motivated to up the intensity to make the most out of your workout.

Dance fitness (all levels welcomed)

Welcome to a freestyle dance class that’ll give you all the benefits of dancing. Every week, you’ll work to master a different energizing choreography, covering all genres of music. Leave class feeling stronger, more agile, and with improved flexibility and coordination. Come along and be constantly surprised by what you might dance to in each class.
